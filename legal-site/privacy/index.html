Privacy Policy

Effective: May 30, 2026 · Last updated: May 30, 2026 · CreatorDraft — TikTok Shop UGC Draft Tool

This Privacy Policy explains how CreatorDraft ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you connect your TikTok account and use our creator workflow tool. By connecting your TikTok account, you agree to the practices described here.

Our service is built on a single principle: you are always in control. No video is ever published to TikTok without your explicit approval inside TikTok's native app. We collect only what is necessary to upload creator-approved drafts. We do not sell data or use it for advertising.

1 Information We Collect

1.1 TikTok Account Information (via OAuth 2.0)

When you authorize CreatorDraft through TikTok's official OAuth 2.0 flow, TikTok grants us limited access tokens. We receive and store:

  • TikTok User ID — a numeric identifier assigned to your account by TikTok
  • Display name — your TikTok @handle, shown in our dashboard so you can confirm the correct account is connected
  • Profile photo URL — a link to your TikTok avatar, used for display purposes only
  • OAuth access token — a short-lived credential that authenticates API calls on your behalf; stored encrypted at rest, never logged
  • OAuth refresh token — used to silently renew access tokens before they expire; stored encrypted at rest, never exposed in URLs or logs

We never receive your TikTok password. Authentication is handled entirely by TikTok's own login system. We see only the access token TikTok issues after you approve our request.

1.2 Content You Create or Input

  • Product names, TikTok Shop URLs, or affiliate links you enter as generation inputs
  • Video scripts, captions, and hashtag sets you write, edit, or approve in our review dashboard
  • Notes or rejection reasons you add to draft decisions

1.3 Generated Content (Temporary)

Our AI pipeline creates the following files on your behalf, stored temporarily:

  • Video files (.mp4) pending your review and approval
  • AI-generated scripts and captions awaiting your decision
  • Thumbnail images created for draft metadata

Generated video files are deleted within 24 hours of upload completion or draft rejection. We do not retain copies of videos after they have been transmitted to TikTok.

1.4 Technical and Log Data

  • Browser type and version (for dashboard compatibility diagnostics)
  • Operating system
  • IP address — used for security and fraud prevention only; not linked to content records
  • Application error logs and API call timestamps — OAuth tokens and credentials are explicitly excluded from all log output
  • Draft upload success/failure status codes

3 How We Share Information

We do not sell, rent, or broker your personal data to any third party. We share your data only in the limited circumstances below.

3.1 TikTok (Required for Service Operation)

When you approve a draft for upload, we transmit the video file and associated metadata (caption, hashtags, privacy settings you select) to TikTok via TikTok's official Content Posting API. This transmission authenticates as your action using your OAuth access token. TikTok's handling of content you upload is governed by TikTok's Privacy Policy.

3.2 Infrastructure Providers

We use the following services, which may process your data on our behalf under data processing agreements:

  • Database hosting (Supabase) — encrypted token and draft queue storage with row-level security
  • Application hosting (Vercel / Render) — serving the web dashboard over HTTPS
  • AI generation (Anthropic Claude API) — generating scripts and captions from product inputs; no TikTok credentials or personal identifiers are sent to the AI provider

These providers do not have independent access to your TikTok OAuth tokens, video files, or personal data outside their specific contracted function.

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by applicable law, valid court order, or governmental authority. Where legally permitted, we will notify you before disclosure occurs.

3.4 Business Transfers

If CreatorDraft is acquired or merged with another entity, your data may transfer to the successor under equivalent privacy protections. We will provide notice at least 14 days before any such transfer occurs.

4 Data Storage and Security

4.1 Encryption

  • OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens are encrypted at rest using AES-256
  • All connections use HTTPS / TLS 1.2 or higher — plaintext HTTP is rejected
  • Credentials and tokens are explicitly excluded from all application log output
  • Environment variables containing secrets are never committed to version control

4.2 Database Architecture

  • Phase 1 (local): Encrypted SQLite database, accessible only to the local application process
  • Phase 2+ (cloud): Supabase with row-level security (RLS) policies ensuring each user can only read or write their own records
  • Database backups are encrypted with the same AES-256 key as production data

4.3 Access Controls

  • OAuth tokens are never exposed in URLs, query strings, client-side JavaScript, or analytics events
  • Tokens are scoped to a single TikTok account per user session
  • Administrative access to the database requires multi-factor authentication
  • No employee or contractor has access to plaintext OAuth tokens

4.4 Incident Response

In the event of a confirmed breach affecting your TikTok credentials, we will notify you within 72 hours and immediately revoke all affected tokens. Re-authorization will be required before service resumes.

6 TikTok Platform Compliance KEY

Official API only: CreatorDraft accesses TikTok exclusively through TikTok's official Content Posting API endpoints. We comply fully with TikTok's Developer Terms of Service, Platform Policy, and all applicable data handling requirements.

6.1 Scopes We Request

CreatorDraft requests only the minimum permissions necessary to operate. At the time of publishing, these are:

  • user.info.basic — to display your TikTok username and profile photo in the review dashboard for account confirmation
  • video.upload — to upload approved video files to your TikTok draft inbox (not published publicly)

We do not request video.publish, video.list, or any other scopes beyond those listed. If we need additional scopes in the future, we will update this policy and request new authorization from you.

6.2 Draft-Only Upload — No Auto-Publishing

The video.upload permission is used exclusively to upload videos to your TikTok draft inbox via the inbox upload endpoint. Drafts are not published automatically. You must open the TikTok app, locate the draft, review it, and tap "Post" manually. CreatorDraft never triggers a public post under any circumstance.

6.3 No Scraping or Unauthorized Access

CreatorDraft does not scrape TikTok.com, TikTok's mobile app, TikTok Shop, or any TikTok property. All TikTok data we access is obtained exclusively through official API endpoints using credentials you have explicitly authorized via OAuth.

6.4 Single-Account Access Only

CreatorDraft only accesses the TikTok account of the user who authorized it. We never access, post to, or manage any TikTok account other than the one the logged-in user personally authenticated via OAuth.

6.5 Revoking Our Access

You can revoke CreatorDraft's TikTok access at any time through either of these paths:

  • In CreatorDraft: Settings → Connected Accounts → Disconnect TikTok
  • In TikTok App: Profile → ··· → Settings and Privacy → Security → Connected Apps → CreatorDraft → Remove Access

Revocation takes effect immediately: all stored tokens are deleted and API access ends permanently until you re-authorize.

6.6 TikTok Data Portability

Any data we receive from TikTok's API is used solely to operate the service on your behalf. We do not combine TikTok-sourced data with data from other platforms for profiling purposes. Upon your request, we will provide or delete all TikTok-derived data we hold about you within 30 days.

7 Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — Request that we correct inaccurate personal data
  • Deletion — Request deletion of all personal data (see below)
  • Portability — Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection — Object to certain processing activities
  • Restriction — Request we limit processing in certain circumstances

7.1 How to Delete Your Data

To request full deletion of your account and all associated data:

  1. Disconnect your TikTok account from the CreatorDraft dashboard (immediately revokes API access)
  2. Email support@creatordraft.app with the subject "Data Deletion Request"
  3. We will confirm deletion of all stored records within 30 days

Note: Deleting your data from CreatorDraft does not delete content already published to TikTok. Content management on TikTok must be done through TikTok's own app or Creator Center.

7.2 Opt-Out of Non-Essential Processing

You may opt out of error log retention by contacting us. This may limit our ability to diagnose issues with your account. You can also limit data collection by disconnecting your TikTok account when not actively using the service.

8 Children's Privacy

CreatorDraft is intended for users who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has provided personal information to us, please contact us at support@creatordraft.app and we will promptly delete that information.
